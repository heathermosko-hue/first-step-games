🐠 How to Play Go Fish ABC!

  1. Goal: Collect the most letter pairs! A pair is an uppercase and lowercase of the same letter — like A + a or B + b.
  2. Blue cards are uppercase letters (A, B, C…). Red/orange cards are lowercase (a, b, c…).
  3. Your turn: Click a letter in your hand, then click "Ask Computer!" to ask for its match.
  4. If the computer has it — you collect the pair and go again! 🎉
  5. If the computer says "Go Fish!" — click the glowing deck to draw a card.
  6. Computer's turn: If it asks for a letter you have, the matching card will glow gold — click it to give it to the computer!
  7. The player with the most pairs when all 26 are found wins!
