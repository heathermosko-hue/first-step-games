📖 How to Play Sight Word Go Fish!

  1. Goal: Collect the most word pairs! A pair is two cards with the same word — like two "the" cards or two "go" cards.
  2. Your turn: Click a word card in your hand to select it, then click "Ask Computer!" to ask if the computer has a matching card.
  3. If the computer has it — you collect the pair and go again! 🎉
  4. If the computer says "Go Fish!" — click the glowing deck to draw a card.
  5. Computer's turn: When the computer asks for a word you have, the matching card will glow gold — click it to give it to the computer!
  6. The game ends when all pairs are found. You always win! 🏆
